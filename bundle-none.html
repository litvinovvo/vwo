Toyota Dealership in Yuma, AZ

Welcome to Alexander Toyota, the dealership that cares. We are located right here in Yuma, and we welcome everyone to come and check us out. Whether you are from Arizona or Southern California, we have the Toyota vehicles that you want and need. With our wonderful selection, you can always find a great vehicle. We even have great Toyota trucks that are perfect for the job-site or the farm. In addition to the Tacoma and the Tundra, we also have a huge lineup of Toyota vehicles including some of your favorites like Camry, Corolla, Prius, RAV4, Highlander, and many more.

Come down to Alexander Toyota, and let us show you what we have to offer. We cater to our customers from miles around, including Ripley, Dome, Somerton, Wellton, San Luis, and even Blythe, Palo Verde, Winterhaven, Date City, and El Centro, California. Don’t forget to check out our selection of used vehicles too. These cars, trucks, and SUVs are the perfect solution to your automotive problems.

Let us show you everything that we are capable of here in Yuma. We even have a Toyota service center that can handle everything from your simple maintenance to the most difficult of repairs. Alexander Toyota is a family-owned dealership, and we are here for our friends and neighbors in Yuma. Stop by and see us today!
